Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Log in at HZ Print and upload the file you want to print. Then go to the printer you want to use and print your document using your HZ card. More information can be found Here.

How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

You can only send documents to your internal HZ email. To do that, register at the printer using your HZ card, then select 'Scanning' and follow the instructions. You will receive the document in your HZ mailbox.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

After signing into your HZ account, you can do it at the Webshop.
Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

You can find the information on the official government website.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Go to the HZ University Helpdesk , and select 'Make a reservation'. You will be able to see the availability schedule of the projects rooms.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

It is currently not possible to park directly at the HZ building in Middelburg
